Unciv is a free, open-source remake of Sid Meier's Civilization V, built with LibGDX. It runs on Android and Desktop (Windows, macOS, Linux), is fully moddable, and is translated into dozens of languages by its community.

Build cities, research technologies, forge alliances, and conquer your enemies — all within a lightweight, fast-running game that works even on low-end hardware.

## What is Unciv?

Unciv faithfully recreates the Civilization V experience as an open-source project. It focuses on:

- **Moddability** — Add nations, units, buildings, terrains, techs, and more through JSON files and the Uniques system
- **Performance** — Runs smoothly on older Android devices and low-spec desktops
- **Community** — Translated into dozens of languages, with an active Discord and modding ecosystem
- **Openness** — Licensed under MPL-2.0, with all development happening in the open on GitHub

<Note>
Unciv gameplay follows Civilization V rules. Any Civ V strategy guide will help you play. The in-game Civilopedia also contains tutorials accessible from the main menu.
</Note>
